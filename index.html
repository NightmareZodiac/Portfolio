About

Dalestair Kidd

I am learning systems, design and media professional with experience across a wide range of media systems and programs within the higher education sector. I have worked in Learning Enhancement and Innovation for the past 5 years and in that time, I have developed high level technical skills to support academics in developing course content and using learning platforms to their full potential.

 I am passionate about accessibility, content creation and user experience. I value my ability to develop innovative and student centric digital learning resources to enhance the student experience.
